Our Service

Our webtool enables you to visualise the SDG data you need in a clear and comprehendible way, allowing you to easily visualise valuable spatial information. Designed to reduce the need for data sifting through large datasets, our dynamic, easy to use web tool allows you to interact with the data, providing decision makers the opportunity to undertake stress-free, in-depth analysis by presenting all the information you need in one place.

About Us

Oliver Brown, Ryan Dossetor, Jack Barrett and Xin Ma are spatial engineering students at the University of Melbourne, combining with CSDILA they have made it their mission to create a web mapping tool to help combat the wicked problems outlined in the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.
